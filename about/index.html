Who is Carlos López Romero?

Feb 09, 2022
2 minute read

Hi I’m Carlos

I’m an Android developer, I currently live in Mexico City. I’m passionate about the technology in mobile development and Linux desktops. I studied a career in “Ciencias de la Informática” at IPN UPIICSA. I also like to create some personal designs using Gravit Design or Figma and I usually plublish them on uplabs.com and instagram.

Currently I’m in progress to get my degree and meanwhile I did the next:

  • I did my Social service at the SECITI (now SECTEI) where I had worked as a Web developer using HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP and frameworks like Angular and Lumen.
  • Consequently, I did my Professional Practice and I continued with my role as a Web developer at SECTEI helping with the maintaining and supporting a internal website.
  • I worked at Lennken Group as a Junior Android Developer where I wrote code for fixing issues, adding new features, and creating new projects.

Lastly I have:

  1. I participated in the AristiComp 2021. It was a competition for Android Developers in which you had to create an app around a topic, the topic was the word “Help”. I build an app called Hazel, whict helps people that are learning English vocabulary to have a useful app where they can search, learn and discover words and phrases. Hazel won 1st place in the AristiComp 2021.

  1. I participated in the HUAWEI Developers LATAM: Winter Contest 2021. Huawei invites developers from Mexico, Peru, Chile, and Colombia to participate in the HUAWEI Developer Latinoamérica Winter Contest to win amazing prizes. It consists create an app and publishing it on the AppGallery additionally the app must integrate at least one of the HMS (Huawei Mobile Services) kits. I implemented some HMS Kits for Hazel like Text-To-Speech, Speech-To-Text, Search, Text recognition, Awareness, Maps, Panorama, and Analitycs. Finally, Hazel won a place, but there isn’t a rating of winners.
