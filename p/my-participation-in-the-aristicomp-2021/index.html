My participation in the AristiComp 2021

May 15, 2022
1 minute read

About the event

I participated in the AristiComp 2021. It was a competition organized by the content creator and Android developer AristiDevs. The hackathon consisted in creating an app around a topic, the topic was the word “Help”.

Details about AristiComp 2021

My participation

I build an app called Hazel, with the purpose of helping people to learn English vocabulary using an app where they can search, learn and discover words and phrases. Hazel won 1st place in the AristiComp 2021.
